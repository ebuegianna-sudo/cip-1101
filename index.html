Welcome to Soul Pen

Soul Pen is a friendly space for those who want to go back in time and revisit memories that still linger. It welcomes readers who question why the world is the way it is and aren’t afraid to sit with those thoughts. Here, curiosity, reflection, and personal experiences are given room to breathe. Ultimately, Soul Pen is for those who simply love to read—and to feel. I am an anonymous computer science student, and this serves as an outlet of my thoughts. Come and join in. Happy Reading!
