Jazz

At Last

Artist: Etta James was a legendary American singer who bridged the gap between rhythm and blues, soul, and jazz. Her deep, earthy voice earned her six Grammy Awards and a permanent place in the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

About the Song: This 1960 soul-jazz ballad serves as the ultimate anthem for finally finding a soulmate after a long period of longing. It is globally recognized for its lush orchestral arrangement and Etta’s iconic, emotive vocal delivery.

Valentine

Artist: Laufey is an Icelandic-Chinese singer-songwriter who has revitalized traditional jazz for Gen Z by blending it with modern pop sensibilities. She is celebrated for her deep, velvety contralto voice and her "modern-day Audrey Hepburn" aesthetic.

About the Song: This charming, acoustic track explores the jittery, sweet anxiety of falling in love for the first time during the Valentine season. It captures the transition from being a cynical "hopeless romantic" to someone who finally has a reason to celebrate love.

Para sa Akin

Artist: Sitti Navarro is the Philippines' "Queen of Bossa Nova," credited with popularizing the Brazilian genre in the local mainstream. Her calm, soothing vocal style has made her a staple in Philippine lounge and easy-listening music.

About the Song: This OPM classic is a gentle declaration of unconditional love and patience regardless of the other person's feelings. Its breezy, rhythmic arrangement makes it a timeless favorite for weddings and romantic playlists.

Pop

Busy Woman

Artist: Sabrina Carpenter is a global pop star known for her clever, "tongue-in-cheek" songwriting and vintage-inspired stage presence. She has successfully transitioned from a teen star to a critically acclaimed artist with a signature witty charm.

About the Song: This upbeat, funky track celebrates a woman who is highly productive and self-sufficient but willing to clear her schedule for the right person. It highlights Sabrina's ability to turn modern dating dynamics into catchy, relatable pop anthems.

intro (end of the world)

Artist: Ariana Grande is one of the most successful vocalists of the 21st century, renowned for her four-octave range and whistle register. Her music often blends R&B, pop, and trap while exploring themes of love, grief, and self-discovery.

About the Song: This ethereal opening track from eternal sunshine poses a philosophical question about the certainty of a partner's love. It sets a dreamy, introspective tone for the album, questioning how one knows if they are truly with the right person.

WHERE IS MY HUSBAND!

Artist: RAYE is a multi-award-winning British independent artist who reclaimed her career after leaving a major label to release jazz and blues-infused pop. She is widely praised for her raw honesty and her ability to blend theatrical storytelling with modern R&B.

About the Song: This bluesy, dramatic track is a humorous yet soulful lament about the exhaustion and absurdity of modern dating. It features RAYE’s powerhouse vocals as she loudly wonders when her search for a serious life partner will finally end.

K-Pop

Life Goes On

Artist: BTS is a world-renowned South Korean boy band known for their diverse musicality and lyrics that address social and personal themes. They have become global icons by fostering a deep emotional connection with their fans, the ARMY.

About the Song: Released during the height of the global pandemic, this alternative hip-hop track offers a message of healing and resilience. It serves as a gentle reminder that even though the world seems to have stopped, life—and the hope for a better day—continues.

Love Wins All

Artist: IU is South Korea’s most beloved solo artist, celebrated for her poetic lyricism and her transition from a "pop idol" to a respected singer-songwriter. She is often called "The Nation's Sweetheart" for her warm personality and consistently chart-topping music.

About the Song: This cinematic ballad is a grand anthem about the power of love to serve as a shield in a cruel and judgmental world. It is famous for its soaring high notes and its high-budget, emotional music video featuring V of BTS.

In My Dreams

Artist: Red Velvet is a unique K-pop girl group known for their "dual" concept: their bright "Red" side and their sophisticated, R&B-focused "Velvet" side. They are highly respected for their intricate vocal harmonies and experimental music videos.

About the Song: This slow-tempo R&B track is a standout for its lush, dream-like atmosphere and melancholic lyrics. It tells the story of wanting to stay asleep because the person they love only exists within their dreams.

R&B

Brown Eyes

Artist: Destiny’s Child is one of the most successful female groups of all time, featuring Beyoncé, Kelly Rowland, and Michelle Williams. They are credited with defining the sound and fashion of early 2000s R&B through their intricate harmonies and empowering lyrics.

About the Song: This tender ballad focuses on the moment of realization when a woman sees her future in her partner’s eyes. It showcases the group’s softer side, relying on vocal purity and a gentle, mid-tempo production.

It's a Wrap

Artist: Mariah Carey is the "Songbird Supreme," legendary for her five-octave range and her status as one of the best-selling female artists ever. Beyond her vocals, she is a prolific songwriter and producer who shaped the modern R&B-pop landscape.

About the Song: This sassy, 1950s-inspired R&B track is a decisive "enough is enough" anthem about cutting off an unreliable partner. It features Mariah’s signature vocal runs and has seen a massive resurgence due to its viral success on social media.

About the Song: This gut-wrenching power ballad is famous for its intense emotional vulnerability and Keyshia’s powerful, soaring high notes. It describes the deep pain and frustration of being in love with someone who doesn't seem to care enough to stay.

OPM

Ewan

Artist: APO Hiking Society was a legendary trio that spearheaded the "Manila Sound" and became a cornerstone of Filipino culture. Their music often focused on humor, national pride, and the relatable experiences of everyday Filipinos.

About the Song: This classic OPM track captures the essence of "Ligawan" (traditional courtship), where a man tries to win over a girl who keeps responding with "Ewan" (I don't know). It remains one of the most popular songs for serenading in the Philippines.

Click To Listen

Bato sa Buhangin

Artist: Cinderella was a prominent band in the 1970s known for their soft, melodic pop sound that defined the "Manila Sound" era. Their lead singer’s gentle vocals became synonymous with the sentimental ballads of that decade.

About the Song: This poetic ballad compares a lasting love to a stone thrown on the sand, questioning if a relationship is permanent or just a passing whim. It is considered one of the most beautiful and nostalgic love songs in the history of OPM.

Click To Listen

Pasilyo

Artist: Sunkissed Lola is a modern Filipino band that gained massive popularity for their soulful blend of indie pop and R&B. They are known for creating "vibe-heavy" music that resonates with the younger generation’s romantic experiences.

About the Song: This viral hit is a heartfelt celebration of the moment a person watches their partner walk down the church aisle (pasilyo). Its soulful melody and romantic lyrics have made it the definitive modern Filipino wedding anthem.
